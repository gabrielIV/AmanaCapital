Savings plans

A. Income investing

Income investing is design for investor who seek regular income and not interested so much in capital appreciation. Investing in income generating investments seeks to preserve capital as a main goal with modest income.

Amana creates an income portfolio of assets specifically tailored to maximize the annual passive cash streams generated by the holdings.
