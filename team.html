Meet the Team

Reginald Kadzutu
Chief Investment Officer
Reginald is charged with providing investment management oversight and overall team leadership. His responsibilities also included providing leadership in the development of new products and proactively managing the Company’s relationship with industry regulators and governmental authorities. Reginald has more than 10 years of experience in investment management, strategy, projects and software implementation and design. He is an ACCA, He holds a BSc Applied accounting from Oxford Brookes University, a Post Graduate Diploma in Investment planning and portfolio management.

Graham Shaw
Chairman
