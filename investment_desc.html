Amana Growth Fund

GROW SAVINGS

Saving Objective:

To generate long term growth of savings above the long-term average inflation for future investment in a tax efficient manner.

Advantages:

  1. High long-term growth above standard saving accounts
  2. Outperforming inflation and government security
  3. Faster growth of savings for bigger investments in future
  4. Easy to liquidate (maximum 3 days)

Who should board this vehicle:

  1. Board this vehicle if you are saving for long-term goals 36 months and beyond
  2. Your risk tolerance is above average
  3. You are below the age of 35
  4. You are a pension fund, a trust or foundation

What do we do with your savings?

Up to 70% of the market value of the fund may be invested in a diversified portfolio of common stocks of companies listed on an organized exchange (such as the Nairobi Stock Exchange). The balance of the fund’s assets shall be invested in short to medium term fixed income securities.

Amana Balanced Fund

ACCUMULATE SAVINGS

Saving Objective:

To accumulate savings, with one eye on moderate growth and the other on reasonable safety of funds. Provides the best of growth fund and shilling fund, growth and monthly income.

Advantages:

  1. High medium-term growth above standard saving accounts
  2. Outperforming inflation and government security
  3. Provides monthly income for accumulating or use to meet monthly needs
  4. Easy to liquidate (maximum 3 days)

Who should board this vehicle:

  1. Board this vehicle if you are saving for medium-term goals 24 months and beyond
  2. You have a moderate risk tolerance
  3. You are below the age of 50
  4. You are a pension fund, a trust or foundation

What do we do with your savings?

At least 40% of the market value of the fund will be invested in a diversified portfolio of Government of Kenya Treasury bonds and corporate bonds. The average term to maturity of the bond holdings will normally be two and five years. At least 20% of the balance of the fund’s assets shall be invested in short term fixed income instruments and At least 40% will be invested in equities listed in a regulated exchange primarily in east, west, south africa.

Amana Shilling Fund

PRESERVE SAVINGS

Saving Objective:

To provide efficient inflation protection on your accumulated savings, with a steady monthly income

Advantages:

  1. Outperforming inflation and government security
  2. Provides monthly income for accumulating or use to meet monthly needs
  3. Easy to liquidate (maximum 3 days)
  4. Provides a vehicle for pension annuities

Who should board this vehicle:

  1. Board this vehicle if you are saving for short-term goals 12 months and below
  2. You want to preserve capital and earn a monthly income
  3. You have a very low risk tolerance
  4. You are above the age of 50
  5. You are a pension fund, a trust or foundation

What do we do with your savings?

The goal of the fund is to achieve a high degree of safety of capital by investing through a diversified portfolio consisting of Treasury Bills issued by the Governments of Kenya, Uganda and Tanzania, commercial paper programs, bank deposits and bankers’ acceptances. The average period to maturity of such securities shall not exceed 12 months.
